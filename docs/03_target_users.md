# Target Users

## Primary Users

### Internal Auditors

Need quick access to audit guidance, risk information, controls, and supporting evidence.

### External Auditors

Need assistance researching audit standards, evaluating disclosures, and identifying relevant audit procedures.

### Audit Managers

Need efficient access to audit guidance and supporting documentation during planning and review activities.

## Secondary Users

### Compliance Professionals

Need to understand audit requirements and supporting controls.

### Finance and Risk Teams

Need access to audit-related guidance and documentation for operational and regulatory purposes.
