# Problem Statement

Audit professionals regularly work with large volumes of standards, regulatory filings, and internal documentation. Finding relevant information often requires manually searching across multiple sources, reviewing lengthy documents, and validating supporting evidence.

This process is time-consuming and can lead to inconsistent research outcomes.

There is an opportunity to improve audit research by using AI to retrieve relevant information, summarize findings, and provide answers supported by citations and source evidence.

AuditLens aims to address this challenge by serving as a centralized audit research assistant that helps users discover, understand, and reference audit-related information more efficiently.
