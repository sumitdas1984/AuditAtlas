# Product Plan

## Target Users

### Primary

- **Internal Auditors** — Need quick access to audit guidance, risk information, controls, and supporting evidence.
- **External Auditors** — Need assistance researching audit standards, evaluating disclosures, and identifying relevant audit procedures.
- **Audit Managers** — Need efficient access to audit guidance and supporting documentation during planning and review activities.

### Secondary

- **Compliance Professionals** — Need to understand audit requirements and supporting controls.
- **Finance and Risk Teams** — Need access to audit-related guidance and documentation for operational and regulatory purposes.

---

## Core Features

- **Audit Standards Research** — Answer questions using authoritative auditing standards.
- **SEC Filing Analysis** — Search and analyze public company disclosures and risk information.
- **Company Document Research** — Retrieve information from internal audit and compliance documentation.
- **Evidence Retrieval** — Identify and present supporting evidence relevant to a user's question.
- **Citation-Supported Answers** — Provide traceable references to source documents used in the response.
- **Multi-Source Research** — Combine information from standards, filings, and company documents when appropriate.

### Out of Scope

Tax research, legal research, accounting standards research, automated audit execution, audit opinion generation.

---

## Success Metrics

AuditLens will be considered successful if it can:

- Retrieve relevant information from trusted sources
- Provide citation-supported answers
- Reduce manual document search effort
- Improve research efficiency
- Maintain transparency through evidence attribution
