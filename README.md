# AuditLens

AuditLens is an AI-powered Audit Research Assistant designed to help audit professionals quickly find, understand, and apply information from auditing standards, SEC filings, and company documents.

The system combines retrieval, reasoning, and source attribution to provide evidence-backed answers with traceable citations. AuditLens aims to reduce the time spent searching through large volumes of documentation while improving transparency and trust in AI-generated responses.

---

## Vision

Audit professionals regularly work with extensive standards, regulatory filings, and internal documentation. Locating relevant guidance and supporting evidence can be time-consuming and often requires searching across multiple sources.

AuditLens serves as an intelligent research companion that helps users:

* Find relevant audit guidance
* Understand audit requirements
* Discover supporting evidence
* Navigate large document collections
* Access citation-supported answers

The goal is not to replace auditor judgment, but to accelerate audit research and evidence discovery.

---

## Problem Statement

Audit research often involves manually reviewing hundreds of pages of standards, filings, and company documents to answer relatively focused questions.

Examples include:

* What audit procedures are required for a specific risk?
* What guidance exists for evaluating internal controls?
* What risks were disclosed in a company's latest annual filing?
* What supporting evidence exists for a particular audit conclusion?

This process is time-consuming and can lead to inconsistent results.

AuditLens addresses this challenge by providing a unified research experience across multiple audit knowledge sources.

## Knowledge Sources

### 1. Auditing Standards

Authoritative audit guidance used as the primary source of truth.

Examples:

* PCAOB Auditing Standards

### 2. SEC Filings

Public company disclosures used as supporting evidence.

Examples:

* 10-K Annual Reports

### 3. Company Documents

Organization-specific documents used for internal research.

Examples:

* Internal Audit Reports
* Risk Registers
* Control Documentation
* Policies and Procedures

## Core Features

### Audit Standards Research

Answer questions using authoritative auditing standards.

### SEC Filing Analysis

Search and analyze public company disclosures and risk information.

### Company Document Research

Retrieve information from internal audit and compliance documentation.

### Evidence Retrieval

Identify and present supporting evidence relevant to a user's question.

### Citation-Supported Answers

Provide traceable references to source documents used in the response.

### Multi-Source Research

Combine information from standards, filings, and company documents when appropriate.

## MVP Scope

The initial MVP focuses on:

* A limited set of PCAOB auditing standards
* A curated collection of SEC 10-K filings
* A small set of company documents
* Retrieval of relevant information
* Citation-supported responses

Out of Scope:

* Tax research
* Legal research
* Accounting standards research
* Automated audit execution
* Audit opinion generation

---

## Success Metrics

AuditLens will be considered successful if it can:

* Retrieve relevant information from trusted sources
* Provide citation-supported answers
* Reduce manual document search effort
* Improve research efficiency
* Maintain transparency through evidence attribution
