# AuditLens

AuditLens is an AI-powered Audit Research Assistant designed to help audit professionals quickly find, understand, and apply information from auditing standards, SEC filings, and company documents.

The system combines retrieval, reasoning, and source attribution to provide evidence-backed answers with traceable citations. AuditLens aims to reduce the time spent searching through large volumes of documentation while improving transparency and trust in AI-generated responses.

## Disclaimer

AuditLens is intended as a research and decision-support tool. All responses should be reviewed and validated by qualified audit professionals before being used in audit, compliance, or regulatory activities.
